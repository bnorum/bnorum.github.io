About

My name is Brady Norum and I am a computer science student at Hofstra University, with a concentration in web and mobile engineering and a minor in math. I graduate in Spring of 2025, and am currently looking for an internship for before or after I graduate. I'm passionate about making the web navigable for creators and those looking to advertise in a digital space. I also have interests in the fields of design and digital art.

This is one of my major creative outlets as a digital artist. As an amatuer musician, I learn from the internet, and just use it to enjoy music. My favorite artist is Steely Dan. I'm probably the biggest Steely Dan fan. I'm also a writer, and plan to publish prose online soon.
