About

My name is Brady Norum and I pretend to be a computer scientist. I'm currently attending Hofstra University for a BA in Computer Science, concentrated in Web and Mobile Engineering. I graduate in 2025, and hopefully I can stop pretending before then (no promises!). I'm passionate about making the web navigable for creators and those looking to advertise in a digital space. I also have interests in the fields of social media and digital art. On my days off, I play accordion, read books (I'm a big David Foster Wallace fan), and get about to a little tomfoolery.
